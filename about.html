Language Preparation

TOPIK Certification for Korean Studies

Learning Korean is essential for your success in Korean universities. We provide comprehensive TOPIK I & II preparation to help you achieve language proficiency.

Why TOPIK Matters

Most Korean universities require proof of Korean language proficiency through the TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) examination. Starting with TOPIK I as a beginner and progressing to TOPIK II as an intermediate learner ensures you can handle academic coursework and university life with confidence.

Our TOPIK Preparation Includes:

  • TOPIK I Beginner Course (8-12 weeks)
  • TOPIK II Intermediate Course (12-16 weeks)
  • 50+ Practice Tests with Answer Keys
  • Self-Paced Learning with Downloadable Materials
  • 3,000+ Vocabulary Words with Pronunciation
  • Audio Listening Practice & Writing Templates
