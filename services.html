Our Services

Everything You Need, Under One Roof

From shortlisting Korean universities to settling into life in Korea, our korea based team supports you at every milestone of your journey to South Korea.

University Selection

We match you with Korean universities and programmes that fit your profile, budget, and career goals — no generic lists.

Admission Guidance

Study plans, SOP, recommendation letters and applications — we polish every detail to make your file stand out to Korean admissions teams.

Student Visa Assistance

D-2 Korean student visa, e-visa documentation, financial proofs and interview prep handled by experts with a 98% visa success record.

Scholarships & Funding

From GKS to university and TOPIK-based scholarships, we identify the funding you qualify for and help you apply the right way.

Pre-Departure Support

Flights, dormitory and accommodation help, packing checklists and Korean culture briefings so you arrive prepared and confident.

Post-Arrival Care

Airport pickup, alien registration, Korean bank account, SIM cards and a friendly local network so you settle into Korea without the stress.

Language Preparation

TOPIK I & II Preparation Course

Master Korean proficiency with our comprehensive TOPIK preparation program. Self-paced learning with downloadable materials for TOPIK I & II certification.

TOPIK I Preparation

Complete beginner-level Korean course (8-12 weeks). Learn Hangul alphabet, basic grammar, essential vocabulary (1,500-2,000 words), and everyday conversation for real-world situations in Korea.

TOPIK II Preparation

Advanced intermediate-level Korean course (12-16 weeks). Master complex grammar, academic writing, reading comprehension, and 3,000-5,000 vocabulary words needed for university-level Korean.

50+ Practice Tests

Prepare with exam-format practice tests for both TOPIK I and TOPIK II levels. Includes detailed answer keys, scoring guidance, and explanations to help you identify weak areas and improve.

Downloadable Materials

Access comprehensive study materials offline. Get PDFs, audio pronunciation files, grammar explanations, vocabulary lists with romanization, and writing templates all in one package.

Self-Paced Learning

Study at your own pace with no deadlines or time limits. Access materials anytime, anywhere. Learn on your schedule while preparing for university in Korea.

Expert Support

Get guidance from Korea-based Korean language experts. Access resources designed by professionals familiar with both Korean education system and international student needs.
